Growth Loop Framework

A systematic approach to identifying and optimizing the core mechanisms that drive sustainable product growth.

Loop Components

Acquisition

How users discover your product

  • Marketing channels
  • Referral systems
  • Content strategy

Activation

How users experience value

  • Onboarding flow
  • Core features
  • Aha moment

Retention

How users stay engaged

  • Engagement hooks
  • Value delivery
  • User habits

Amplification

How users spread the word

  • Viral features
  • Network effects
  • Social proof

ICE Score Framework

A simple but effective framework for prioritizing growth initiatives based on Impact, Confidence, and Ease.

Scoring Components

Impact

Potential effect on key metrics

1

Minimal impact

5

Medium impact

10

High impact

Confidence

Certainty in the outcome

1

Low confidence

5

Medium confidence

10

High confidence

Ease

Implementation difficulty

1

Very difficult

5

Moderate effort

10

Very easy

Hook Model

A framework for building habit-forming products through a four-phase process that connects your solution to users' problems and emotions.

Hook Cycle

Trigger

External
  • Notifications
  • Emails
  • Ads
Internal
  • Emotions
  • Routines
  • Situations

Action

  • Simple behavior
  • Anticipation of reward
  • Minimal effort required

Variable Reward

Types
  • Tribe (Social)
  • Hunt (Resources)
  • Self (Mastery)

Investment

  • Store value
  • Improve service
  • Build commitment
